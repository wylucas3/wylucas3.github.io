Tips for New Hikers

  • Start small!

    Choose the right trail for your fitness level. There's nothing like an easy walk through the woods to get you hooked!

  • Check the weather.

    Sunny days are the BEST, but cold or rain can be kind of a bummer if you're unprepared.

  • Wear the right shoes.

    Blisters are a sure way to kill a hike. I recommend Hokas.

  • Leave no trace.

    These beautiful trails you will grow to love won't be here long if we don't take care of them!
