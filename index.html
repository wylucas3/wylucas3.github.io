Why I love hiking

My life can get pretty crazy. Trying to balance work, school, family, friends, sports, and so on takes up a LOT of time and energy! I know you get it. We all have a lot on our plate. Last year, in an effort to chill out and take a break from this craziness, I discovered hiking. Now I'm certainly not saying that I'm an expert hiker, or even a good hiker! But being outside exploring our beautiful Earth has become one of my favorite passtimes.

My favorite hikes

Name Difficulty Level Description
Pinnacle Beginner Wooded trails, beautiful view overlooking Post Pond, need I say more?!
Gile Beginner If you're not much into hiking, then Gile is perfect for you! The trail is super well-groomed, and it's got a beautiful 360 view at the top!
Mt. Cube Advanced I've only done this hike once. It was a serious workout getting to the top, but make sure you bring extra layers! It can get chilly way up there.
